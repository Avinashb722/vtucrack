Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

At VTUCrack (www.vtucrack.com), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may provide while using our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect limited personal information when you interact with our site, specifically if you use our contact forms or request materials. This includes:

  • Name and Email Address
  • Usage data (IP address, browser type) via Google Analytics
  • Cookies for personalized experience

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to enhance your experience. We do not sell your data to third parties. We use it to:

  • Respond to notes requests and queries
  • Improve our educational resource selection
  • Analyze traffic patterns to optimize site speed

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to understand how visitors interact with VTUCrack. This helps us provide faster load times and relevant results. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may behave differently.

4. Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.

5. External Links

Our resources often link to external PDF viewers (Google Drive) or university portals. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or content of these third-party services.
